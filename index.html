Welcome to Growth and Inspiration through Volunteering and Education!

A bit about us...

Growth and Inspiration through Volunteering and Education (GIVE), is a non-profit organization founded and run by high school students serving the Washington D.C. metropolitan area. Our mission is to cultivate leadership among youth, primarily through a free tutoring program, as well as other activities. GIVE invests in the next generation of change-makers by providing opportunities in the fields of education, empowerment, and leadership.

We work with different public facilities across the DC Metropolitan area to hold tutoring sessions, join our family!
